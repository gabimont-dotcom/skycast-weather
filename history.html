History of Weather Forecasting

Weather prediction has evolved from folklore and observation to modern satellite technology. In the 19th century, the invention of the telegraph allowed for the first real-time sharing of weather data. Today, we use global weather models powered by supercomputers to predict storms, temperatures, and climate patterns.
